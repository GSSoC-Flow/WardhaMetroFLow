Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Wardha Metro services

Wardha Metro operates daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Timings may vary slightly during public holidays and maintenance schedules.

Tickets can be purchased through ticket vending machines, metro stations, or the Wardha Metro mobile app for cashless transactions.

Yes, discounted travel passes are available for students and senior citizens. Visit the nearest metro counter with valid ID proof to apply.

Small personal luggage is allowed. However, heavy or oversized items are restricted to ensure passenger comfort and safety.

Please reach out to the Wardha Metro Customer Service at 1800-123-4567 or visit the Lost & Found counter at your nearest metro station.
